The Science Behind the Taper

Unlike some many parts of marathon training, the science around tapering is actually pretty solid. It works. Studies show that reducing training volume and intensity in the final weeks leads to better race-day performance. Makes sense, One widely cited meta-analysis suggests that an exponential taper, where training volume is cut more sharply as race day approaches, is especially effective.(Bosquet et al. 2007)
